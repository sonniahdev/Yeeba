Who We Are

At Yeeba Impact, we believe that impactful decisions begin with understanding. We empower organizations to turn raw data into transformative action by putting human experiences at the heart of every data endeavor.

Picture this: an organization flooded with information but unsure how to channel it into change. That's where we step in. With a blend of analytical rigor and strategic foresight, we dive deep into your organizational data to uncover the stories it tells. We bridge the gap between information and action, ensuring that every decision you make amplifies your social impact.

What We Do

Impact Diagnosis

Imagine trying to solve a puzzle without seeing the full picture. That's what addressing community needs without a proper diagnosis feels like. At Yeeba Impact, we start with a comprehensive needs assessment, delving into the heart of the communities and users you serve. By employing systems thinking, we don't just look at individual problems—we explore how they connect, interact, and influence one another.

Impact Prognosis

Once the diagnosis is clear, it's time to chart a path forward. Crafting interventions isn't about imposing solutions; it's about designing programs that resonate deeply with the communities you serve. Employing user-centered approaches, we co-create solutions that are not only practical but also culturally and socially relevant.

What We Do

Impact Monitoring

Good intentions are only as strong as their execution. As your initiatives roll out, we ensure they remain on track through rigorous monitoring. Imagine having a dashboard that shows not just what's happening but why it's happening. That's the level of clarity we bring. This allows you to track progress in real-time and adjust your approach when necessary.

Impact Evaluation

When the work is done, how do you measure its success? At Yeeba Impact, we go beyond surface-level metrics to uncover the true depth of your impact. Using experimental and quasi-experimental methods, we analyze the tangible and intangible outcomes of your projects. We don't just tell you what worked—we help you understand why it worked.
